# Quick style panels

**Quick style panels** provide quick access to the most common layout properties directly in the **layout builder**. Rather than opening the full inspector panel, you configure borders, backgrounds, display mode, and positioning from a dedicated panel.

## Where to find them

SCREEN_HERE: quick style panels in the layout builder

Select an element to display the quick style panels. Each panel groups the properties of a category.

## Borders

The border panel allows you to quickly configure the border style of your element. Adjust the thickness, color, pattern, and rounding.

## Background

The background panel controls the appearance of the background. Apply a solid color, gradient, or image.

## Display

The display panel defines how your element participates in the document flow. Choose the display mode and configure the behavior in case of overflow.

## Positioning

The positioning panel allows you to place your element precisely relative to its containers or neighbors.
